My top 3 Pixar Movies

Pixar Studios is one of the most renowned animators in the world and they have made many amazing movies. Here are my top 3 favorite Pixar movies and why I like them so much.

#1 My Favorite Movie

Wall-E

My favorite Pixar movie of all time is Wall-E. I have always been a fan of Sci-Fi films and Wall-E is a great example of one. I love the allegory of what the world will look like if we don't take care of it and that it will get so bad humans' technology won't be able to take care of the trash so they will end up having to leave the planet after destroying all of nature. The story is great, the robots are cool, and it has a very nice ending.

#2 My Second Pick

Finding Nemo

If you asked anyone what their top 3 favorite Pixar movies are, I bet that almost everyone would have Finding Nemo on that list. This movie has such a good story and characters all while it showcases amazing animation of sea life. As someone who loves the ocean, Finding Nemo portrays the ocean and its life beautifully and almost makes it seem like a whole different world but its all on our home planet.

#3 My Third Choice

The Incredibles

The Incredibles is the very definition of a classic. This movie holds a special place in my heart being the first super hero movie I watched as a kid and despite being almost 20 years old now, it is still an amazing movie. The story and characters are really good and the super powers along with the action scenes are captivating. Even though I am not much of a super hero fan anymore, The incredibles is still a great movie that I can enjoy to this day.
